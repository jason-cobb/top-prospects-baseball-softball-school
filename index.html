About Top Prospects of Louisville

Top Prospects of Louisville Baseball and Fastpitch Softball School emerged from the clients that Aja and JB had built for years.
After working with the legend Mike Baldwin at Future Pros, the opportunity presented to open Top Prospects in a more central location.
We are dedicated to providing athletes quality instruction, training them to produce quality results, and helping them become leaders on and off the field.
Our Mission is to train athletes to Learn, Grow, and Dream Big in the game of Softball and Baseball.

  • Learn correct mechanics and improve the mental aspect of the game to gain an advantage over opponents.
  • Grow in knowledge, talent, and grit to over come adversity.
  • Dream Big to shape their futures that will set them apart not only on the field but in life.
These Athletes are in good hands with our combined experience and knowledge of the game of Baseball and Softball.
There are two things you can control at all times, Effort and Attitude!

Get to Know the Team

Aja
Ms. Aja Sherman is originally from Las Vegas Nevada and started playing when she was 11 years old. She Played for various travel ball teams and was eventually recruited and signed to play First Base/Pitcher for the University of Louisville in Kentucky, 2002–2005.
During her time at UofL she splitted playing time between First Base and Pitching, and was named All Conference First Team twice.
Aja was named Conference USA Pitcher of the Year in 2005. She is the only UofL softball pitcher to have pitched a Perfect Game. She was the first UofL player to play professional softball, playing for the Arizona Heat in 2005 amongst many Olympic athletes. She also played over seas, playing for Miramar in Wellington New Zealand.
Because of her deep love for the game and competitive nature she currently still plays in the Lyndon 18 over fast pitch league in Louisville, where she plays amongst several of the players she coached and currently coaches. Aja has been teaching Pitching since 2006, has helped coach at the UofL Camps and Clinics, was an assistant coach at Sacred Heart Academy 2007-2008, coached while over seas in New Zealand 2008-2009, and has held camps and clinics throughout her coaching career.
She looks forward to helping more athletes gain a better understanding of the game of softball and to help them improve, grow, and stay competitive throughout their careers.

JB
Mr. James Brentlinger “ JB ” is a certified Epstein hitting instructor and was an assistant to the legend Mike Baldwin at Future Pro for 6 years.
He has been coaching softball since 2002. He has coached: Fern Creek Babe Ruth 2002- 2005, 5 Softball Travel Teams 2005 -2012, DuPont Manual High School Assistant Coach 2010 - 2013, Derby City Crush 16u 2016, Lady Sluggers 16U 2018, and was the Head Coach at Jeffersontown High School. While at Jeffersontown High School he has led the team to the most victories in school history since 2001 and has also made it into the KHSAA Record books during the same season when J-Town as a team posted 25 Homeruns on the season, for that he was voted 6th Region Coach of the Year by his peers.
He is also currently the Head of the KC Freedom Kentucky Travel Softball and Head Coach of Mercy Academy Softball program.
